The method

Structure first. Then write.

Every scene generated knows exactly what it needs to do — dramatically, emotionally, structurally. No more blank page. No more drift.

01
Paste what you have

A synopsis, a treatment, a messy document. Paste it in. The platform reads it and extracts everything: characters, events, themes, turning points, open threads.

02
Choose your arc

Three-act, five-part dramatic arc, or a custom structure. Your story gets mapped to clusters — each one annotated with its dramatic and emotional function.

03
Write cluster by cluster

Move through your story scene by scene. The AI generates prose that knows its structural role. You read, react, steer, accept. Your authorship stays intact.

04
Stay on arc. Or change it.

The system holds the overarching structure as you write. If you want to edit the clusters, you can — from the back end, with full control. Nothing breaks.

05
Export your draft

When you're ready, export your accepted scenes as a formatted manuscript. Clean Word document, scene by scene. Yours to take anywhere.
